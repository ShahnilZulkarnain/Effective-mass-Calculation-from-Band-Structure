Effective Mass Calculator

Analyze electronic band structures — band gap, VBM/CBM, and effective masses. Runs entirely in your browser, no install.

Drop a CSV file here
or click to browse
CSV format (no header):
• 2 columns: k, energy(eV)
• 4 columns: k1, E1, k2, E2 (two spin components, concatenated)
k-points run in Brillouin-zone units from 0 to 1. Energies are shifted so the Fermi level is at 0 eV.
